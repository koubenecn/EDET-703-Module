Part 1.2: Meeting the staff

You decide to stop by to find answers to some of your questions.

When you arrive at the office, the study abroad staff welcome you and introduce themselves:


  • Zane Segle, Director
    Zane is responsible for reviewing requests for waivers of study abroad GPA or conduct requirements.

  • Ashley Dudley, Assistant Director
    Ashley manages the online application portal, citadel.abroadoffice.net, as well as the Global Scholars programs in Greece and Cyprus. She also advises students on how to select study abroad programs and plan for study abroad.

  • Amie Parris, Program Coordinator
    Amie advises students considering study abroad, provides support with passports and visa applications and travel, and manages the Global Scholars programs in Rome and Wales.

  • Noah Koubenec, Assistant Director
    Noah is responsible for the Project GO program in Taiwan as well as advising applicants for competetive scholarships and fellowships like the Rhodes Scholarships.
