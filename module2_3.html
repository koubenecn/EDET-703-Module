Part 2.3: The Transient Approval Form

For courses not listed on collegetransfer.net, you will need to submit a transfer approval request form, which allows the relevant academic department to verify the study abroad course you'd like to transfer will appear on your transcript as the course you need.

When completing the transfer credit approval form, pay special attention to the key sections highlighted in yellow. Click on the sections for additional detail on each.

This column should be used to list the academic calendar (semester or quarter) at the non-Citadel institution.

Quarter systems consists of four 10-week sessions in the fall, winter, spring, and summer.
