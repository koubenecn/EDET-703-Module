Part 2.2: Meet with your academic advisor

Before applying to any study abroad program, you should discuss your plans with your academic advisor.

Your conversation should include a discussion of what courses are available on the study abroad program and how those would fit within your degree plan.

Keep it mind that if you are planning to study abroad in a given term, it may be possible to take courses for that term which are different from those you would take on campus, while still making satisfactory progress on your degree.
