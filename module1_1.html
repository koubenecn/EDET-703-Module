Part 1.1: Where to find the office

You are interested in studying abroad, but don't understand how to find programs that are available or what the application process is. However, you do remember the study abroad website from a conversation with a classmate who studied abroad last semester.

When you visit www.citadel.edu/studyabroad, you find that the study abroad office is located on 202 Richardson Avenue, directly behind Bond Hall.
