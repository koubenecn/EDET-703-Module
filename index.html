Part 1.0: About this module

This web-based learning module will guide you through a study abroad application scenario. You will learn:

  1. Where to find study abroad staff on campus,
  2. The names and roles of office of study abroad staff,
  3. How to apply to study abroad online,
  4. And how to ensure you get the course credit you need on your study abroad program.
