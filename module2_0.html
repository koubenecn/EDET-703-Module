Part 2.0: Understanding Transfer Credit

You send an email to Ashley Dudley, who reminds you that there a number of factors which affect how you receive credit for study abroad courses. Some programs offer Citadel courses and will appear on your transcript just like courses you take on campus, while many others will need to be transferred, which means you should verify what courses they will transfer as before you study abroad. She explains that there are several different steps to making sure you will get the course credits you need on a study abroad program:

  1. Visit collegetransfer.net to identify courses offered on the study abroad program and whether they are pre-approved to transfer back to The Citadel. Many courses offered on study abroad programs have already been approved for transfer and are listed on this site.
  2. Meet with your academic adviser (be sure to bring a list of all courses offered on the study abroad program!)
  3. For courses not listed on collegetransfer.net, you will need to submit a transfer approval request form, which allows the relevant academic department to verify the study abroad course you'd like to transfer will appear on your transcript as the course you need.
